What This Means

COVID-19: The AI model predicts this X-ray shows patterns consistent with COVID-19 infection.

Common radiological findings in COVID-19 include:

  • Ground glass opacities, often bilateral
  • Peripheral and lower zone predominance
  • Consolidation in later stages

Lung Opacity: The AI model detects lung opacities which represent an area of hazy lung density.

Lung opacities can be caused by:

  • Pneumonia (various types)
  • Pulmonary edema
  • Hemorrhage
  • Various other lung conditions

Normal: The AI model predicts this X-ray appears normal without significant abnormalities.

Normal chest X-rays typically show:

  • Clear lung fields
  • Normal cardiac silhouette
  • No significant opacities or consolidations

Viral Pneumonia: The AI model detects patterns consistent with viral pneumonia.

Common findings in viral pneumonia include:

  • Bilateral interstitial patterns
  • Patchy or diffuse airspace opacities
  • Rarely presents with lobar consolidation

About Chest X-ray Analysis

Chest X-rays are one of the most common imaging tests in medicine, providing valuable information about the lungs, heart, and chest wall.

Our AI model is trained to identify four different conditions in chest X-rays:

  • COVID-19: Lung patterns consistent with COVID-19 infection
  • Lung Opacity: Hazy areas in the lungs that could indicate various conditions
  • Normal: No significant abnormalities detected
  • Viral Pneumonia: Patterns consistent with pneumonia caused by viral infection
