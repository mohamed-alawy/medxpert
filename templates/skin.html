About Skin Cancer Classification

Skin cancer is one of the most common types of cancer worldwide. Early detection is key to successful treatment, with a high cure rate for skin cancers detected in early stages.

Our AI model analyzes images of skin lesions and classifies them as benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). The model has been trained on thousands of dermatologist-labeled images to provide accurate classifications.
